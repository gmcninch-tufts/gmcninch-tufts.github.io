ARTIN workshop

Posted on 2017-09-15
Tags: algebraic-groups, workshop

The Workshop on Lie Theory, Representation Theory and Algebraic Groups was held at the University of Manchester (UK) Sept 11-14, 2017 as part of the series “Algebra and Representation Theory in the North” (ARTIN).

I contributed a lecture on the Nilpotent orbits of a reductive group over a local field.
