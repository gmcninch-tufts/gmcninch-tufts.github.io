Posted on 2018-04-23

The Spring Eastern Sectional Meeting of the AMS was held at Northeastern University (Boston, MA) April 21-22, 2018.

At this meeting, there was a special session on Combinatorial Aspects of Nilpotent Orbits organized by Anthony Iarrobino (Northeastern University), Leila Khatami (Union College) and Juliana Tymoczko (Smith College).
